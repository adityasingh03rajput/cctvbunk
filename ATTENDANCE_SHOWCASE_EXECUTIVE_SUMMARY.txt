================================================================================
                        KEY CALCULATIONS NEEDED
================================================================================

1. OVERALL ATTENDANCE %
   Formula: (Days Present / Total Working Days) * 100
   Working Days = Days with classes (exclude weekends/holidays)
   Days Present = Count of days with status = "present"

2. SUBJECT-WISE %
   Formula: (Classes Attended / Total Classes) * 100
   Total Classes = Count of periods with that subject
   Classes Attended = Count of periods with status = "present"

3. TEACHER'S CLASS ATTENDANCE
   Formula: (Students Present / Total Students) * 100
   Total Students = Count of unique students in semester+branch
   Students Present = Count of students with status = "present" on date
